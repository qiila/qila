Biodata of Nur Akilah

My name is Nur Akilah Binti Che Abdullah, and I am a 20-year-old student currently pursuing my studies in Diploma of Information Management at UiTM , Rembau campus. I am enrolled in the PSM,KPPIM As the eighth child out of nine siblings, I come from a large and close-knit family that has always inspired me to strive for excellence. Growing up in a caring family with many siblings taught me the importance of teamwork, patience, and adaptability. Despite the difficulties that come with being part of a large family, I've always been encouraged to follow my goals and make the most of my education. From a young age, I was an ambitious person who set goals personally and worked hard to attain them. I feel that determination is essential for overcoming hurdles and realizing my best potential.

As the American author and motivational speaker, Zig Ziglar, once said, “You don’t have to be great to start, but you have to start to be great.” This quote inspires me to take the first step toward my dreams, no matter how challenging it may seem.

At present, my main ambition is to continue my studies and complete my degree. I am passionate about learning and have a deep interest in the field of Information Management, as it offers a perfect blend of technology, data, and organizational skills. As I work toward achieving this goal, I always keep in mind the words of the renowned writer, J.K. Rowling: “It takes a great deal of bravery to stand up to our enemies, but just as much to stand up to our friends.” To me, this quote reflects the courage required to pursue one’s dreams, even in the face of challenges or doubts.
